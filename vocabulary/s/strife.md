# strife

- Word: strife

- Type: noun [U]
- Meaning: angry or violent disagreement between two people or groups of people
- Chinese: 冲突；争斗
- Tags: relation::bad
- Eg.: 20 years of civil strife have left the country's economy in ruins.
- Eg.: civil strife 内乱
- Eg.: The country was torn apart by strife. 这个国家被内部纷争搞得四分五裂。
