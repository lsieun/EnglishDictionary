# reincarnate

- Word: reincarnate

- Type: verb [I]
- Meaning: to be born again in another body after you have died; to make sb be born again in this way
- Chinese: 使投胎；转世；使再生
- Tags: 
- Similar: incarnate
- Use: ~ (sb/sth) (in/as sb/sth)
- Eg.: They believe humans are reincarnated in animal form. 他们相信人死后转生为动物。
