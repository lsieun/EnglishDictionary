# contemplation

- Word: contemplation

## noun

- Meaning: the act of thinking deeply about sth
- Chinese: 深思；沉思
- Tags: think
- Synonyms: meditation
- Eg.: He sat there deep in contemplation. 他坐在那里沉思着。
- Eg.: a few moments of quiet contemplation 默默沉思的片刻
- Eg.: a life of prayer（祈祷的行为） and contemplation 祈祷与冥思的生活
