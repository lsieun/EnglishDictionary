# infamous

- Word: infamous

- Type: adjective
- Meaning: well known for being bad or evil
- Chinese: 臭名远扬的；声名狼藉的
- Tags: 
- Synonyms: notorious
- Eg.: a general who was infamous for his brutality 因残忍而恶名昭彰的将军
- Eg.: the most infamous concentration camp 最恶名远扬的集中营
- Eg.: the infamous British sandwich 以难吃著名的英国三明治
