# bacon

- Word: bacon

## noun

- Meaning: meat from the back or sides of a pig that has been cured(腌,熏) (= preserved using salt or smoke), usually served in thin slices
- Chinese: 培根；熏肉；咸肉
- Tags: food, meat
- Eg.: a rasher of bacon 一片咸猪肉
- Eg.: bacon and eggs 咸肉和鸡蛋
