# workaround

- Word: workaround

- Type: noun [C]
- Meaning: a way of dealing with a problem or making something work despite the problem, without completely solving it
- Chinese: 权变措施；变通方案
- Tags: problem::solve
- Eg.: The software still has a few glitches(小故障；小毛病) but we've come up with a workaround.
