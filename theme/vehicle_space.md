# vehicle

- spacecraft: 航天（飞行）器；宇宙飞船；太空船 a vehicle that travels in space
- space station: 太空站；航天站；宇宙空间站 a large structure that is sent into space and remains above the earth as a base for people working and travelling in space
- astronaut: 宇航员；航天员 a person whose job involves travelling and working in a spacecraft
